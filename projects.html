Design of a 1200 TPD Ammonia Production Plant

April 2023

In this capstone project, we designed a complete ammonia production facility capable of producing 1200 tons per day.

Key Steps:

  • Process Selection: Chose the Haldor Topsoe process for its efficiency and suitability for large-scale ammonia production.
  • Design Work: Created process flow diagrams, performed material and energy balances, and sized key equipment like reformers, ammonia converters and CO2 absorbers.
  • Environmental Planning: Developed strategies to manage air, water, and solid waste pollution.
  • Hysys simulation
  • Individual equipment design and sizing (Primary Reformer)
  • Economic analysis in the context of Bangladesh

Modeling and Optimization of Biodiesel Production from Caper Spurge

April 2022

Objective: Designed and optimized a biodiesel production process from Caper Spurge (Euphorbia lathyris) oil using Aspen HYSYS simulation software.

Feedstock: Utilized non-edible Caper Spurge oil as a sustainable and renewable raw material.

Process: Modeled the transesterification reaction using methanol and a basic catalyst to produce biodiesel and glycerol.

Simulation: Developed a steady-state process model in Aspen HYSYS including reaction kinetics, separation units, and recycle streams.

Optimization Goals:

  • Maximize biodiesel yield.
  • Minimize methanol consumption and energy usage.

Key Achievements:

  • Achieved an optimized biodiesel yield (92% conversion rate) with reduced excess methanol.
  • Demonstrated feasibility of Caper Spurge oil as a viable biodiesel feedstock.

Sustainability: Contributed to green energy initiatives by promoting biodiesel from non-food, drought-tolerant crops.
