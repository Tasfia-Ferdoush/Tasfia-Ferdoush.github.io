Modeling and Optimization of Biodiesel Production from Caper Spurge

April 2022

This project focused on simulating a biodiesel production plant using Aspen HYSYS. The goal was to optimize the conversion process from caper spurge oil to biodiesel via transesterification. The final design achieved a 92% conversion rate, with key attention to reaction kinetics, separation stages, and sensitivity analysis of feed ratios.
